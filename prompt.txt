Fix two confirmed bugs from ADB logs. Bug 1 the media permission popup: after stopping recording the app shows a permission dialog from com.google.android.providers.media.PermissionActivity for media write access. This is separate from the audio permission. Fix this by requesting WRITE_EXTERNAL_STORAGE and media write permissions at app startup in _layout.tsx alongside the existing MediaLibrary permissions so no permission dialogs can appear after recording stops. Bug 2 audio silenced when switching apps: ADB logs show App op 27 missing silencing record exactly 5 seconds after the app loses foreground. The foreground service is not maintaining audio recording privileges. The fix must use a proper Android ForegroundService with foreground service type microphone declared in the manifest. Use expo-task-manager to register a background task that keeps the recording alive and prevents the OS from silencing it. The service must start when recording begins and stop when recording ends. This requires a new native build. Run all tests and confirm they pass.
